    Github Stale Branch Blamer. Find those unused branches, prune them, or annoy their owners.

    GSBB: The easiest way to prune your remote branches!

    Usage:

    gsbb show -- display all old, unused, stale, remote branches

    gsbb email -- display the list of offending email addresses

    gsbb prune -- i'm crazy: just delete what you think is stale.
